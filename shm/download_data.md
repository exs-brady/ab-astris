# SHM Data Access

## Z24 Bridge Dataset

The Z24 Bridge benchmark dataset is available from KU Leuven:

> De Roeck, G. et al. (2000). "Z24 Bridge Damage Detection Tests."

**Access**: Request the dataset from the KU Leuven Structural Mechanics
division. The data includes acceleration recordings from 14 sensors over
a 10-month progressive damage campaign (1997-1998).

Place the Matlab files in `z24/data/`:

```
shm/z24/data/
├── z24_frequencies.mat
└── z24_damage_labels.mat
```

The pre-computed results in `z24/results/z24_damage_predictions.csv`
allow reproducing Figure 2 and Section 3.5.2 without the raw data.

## CESMD Building Data

Strong-motion recordings from the Center for Engineering Strong Motion
Data (CESMD):

**Access**: https://www.strongmotioncenter.org/

Search for instrumented buildings with ambient vibration or earthquake
response data. The experiment in Section 3.5.1 uses recordings from
the CESMD public archive.

## LUMO Lattice Tower

The LUMO benchmark uses synthetic data generated by `shm_data.py` —
no external download is required.
